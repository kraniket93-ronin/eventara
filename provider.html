About Paandora Grand Udaipur

Paandora Grand Udaipur is a full-service banquet hotel on the Udaipur–Ahmedabad Highway at Balicha, with 100 rooms overlooking the Aravalli hills, indoor banquet and conference halls, lush green wedding lawns, and the open-air Sunset Deck. One team looks after your whole event — the venue, in-house catering, guest rooms, sound & staging, and on-the-day coordination — all in a single booking.

From grand weddings and birthdays to corporate offsites, conferences and award nights, the property hosts celebrations of every size. A spacious banquet hall, breakaway rooms, wedding lawns and two pools make it just as good for an intimate family function as for a 500-guest reception — with dedicated staff to make sure the day runs beautifully.
