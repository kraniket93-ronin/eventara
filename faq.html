General

Eventara is Udaipur's trusted online marketplace for planning events of every kind - weddings, birthdays, corporate days, conferences and celebrations. We connect people planning events with verified venues, hotels and event planners, so you can discover options, compare clear quotes, and book with your payment protected until your event is done.

Tell us about your event in a short form, and we match your request to a few verified venues or planners. Their quotes arrive within 48 hours, lined up side by side so you can compare fairly. Pick the one you like, pay a small deposit to confirm, and your payment stays safe with us until the event is delivered.

Both sides of an event. Customers - families, companies and institutions planning any occasion - use Eventara to find and book trusted suppliers. Businesses - hotels, banquet venues and event planners - use it to reach genuine customers and grow their bookings.

Yes. It's completely free for customers to browse, request quotes and compare. Businesses can list for free too - Eventara earns only a small commission on confirmed bookings, with no listing or sign-up fees.

Booking & Events

Browse listings or submit a quote request. Once quotes arrive, compare them, choose the one that fits, and pay a small deposit to confirm. Your booking, invoice and all details are then saved to your account.

Use the “Get Free Quotes” form and tell us your event type, date, guest count and budget. Your request goes straight to a few matched venues and planners who reply with clear, itemised quotes.

Each business sets its own pricing based on your requirements - guest count, date, inclusions and services. You receive itemised quotes so you can see exactly what's included and compare fairly.

Yes. You can message a business through Eventara to discuss inclusions, tweak a package or ask about pricing. Your contact details stay private until you decide to book.

Absolutely. You can request quotes from several venues and planners, shortlist and compare them, and book more than one supplier for the same event - for example a venue plus a separate planner or caterer.

Open My Bookings in your account and choose the booking you want to change. Modifications and cancellations follow the terms shown on that booking; any charges depend on how far in advance you cancel.

Your request is sent to the matched businesses right away. Their quotes usually arrive within 48 hours, and we line them up side by side so you can compare and choose without any back-and-forth.

A booking is confirmed the moment you accept a quote and pay the deposit. The business is notified immediately, and your deposit is held safely by Eventara until your event takes place.

Payments

You can pay by UPI (GPay, PhonePe, BHIM), net banking or major debit/credit cards. Businesses receive their payouts directly to their registered bank account.

A small deposit - usually around 30% - confirms your booking and shows the business you're committed. It's held safely by Eventara, not paid straight to the supplier, and the balance is settled as per your booking terms.

Refunds follow each booking's cancellation terms, which are shown to you before you pay. Cancel far enough in advance and your deposit is refundable in full or in part.

Charges depend on timing. A typical policy is a full refund 30+ days before the event, a partial refund 15–30 days before, and no refund under 15 days - but you never pay the balance. The exact terms appear on each booking.

Yes. Quotes show applicable GST, and you receive a proper GST invoice along with a complete booking record for every confirmed booking - easy to download and keep for yourself or your company.

Very. Your money is held safely by Eventara and released to the business only after your event is delivered. All transactions run through secure payment gateways, and money never moves off-platform.

On the Sign In page, click “Forgot Password?” and follow the instructions sent to your email to set a new one.

Go to My Account and open Profile to update your name, contact details, GST number and saved preferences at any time.

Reach out to support from the Help section and we'll close your account and remove your data in line with our Privacy Policy. Some records may be retained where the law requires it.

Hotels & Venues

Register as a business, add your property details, photos, halls and rooms, and set your pricing. Your listing goes live once verification is complete.

From your dashboard you can add each hall or room with its capacity, inclusions and pricing, and edit them any time.

Edit your package tiers and per-event pricing directly in the dashboard - changes apply to new quotes straight away.

Mark dates as available or blocked on your calendar so you only receive enquiries for open dates and avoid double-bookings.

Accept a request by sending a quote. Once the customer pays the deposit, the booking is confirmed and appears in your dashboard with all the details.
